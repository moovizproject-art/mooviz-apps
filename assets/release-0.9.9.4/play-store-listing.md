### English (en-US)

```
MOOVIZ — Israel's Community Delivery Platform

Need to send a package? Looking for a driver? MOOVIZ connects senders with community drivers in your area, featuring live tracking, built-in chat, and photo proof of pickup and delivery.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚀 How It Works

As a Sender:
• Post a new delivery with a photo, pickup address, and destination
• Receive offers from nearby community drivers
• Approve your preferred driver and track in real time
• Get photo proof of pickup and delivery
• Confirm payment via Bit, PayBox, or bank transfer

As a Driver:
• Browse available deliveries near you
• Express interest and get approved by the sender
• Navigate to the pickup point with a live map
• Take proof-of-pickup and proof-of-delivery photos
• Receive payment directly from the sender

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✨ Key Features

📍 Real-Time Tracking
Live map showing the driver's location in real time. Senders know exactly where their package is at every moment.

💬 Built-in Chat
Direct communication between sender and driver inside the app — no need for WhatsApp or phone calls.

📸 Photo Proof System
Complete proof chain: pickup photo, delivery photo, and payment confirmation — everything documented.

🔔 Real-Time Notifications
Updates at every stage: driver interested, sender approved, picked up, delivered, payment confirmed, and more.

⭐ Ratings & Reviews
Rate your delivery experience and choose drivers with high ratings.

🔒 Driver Verification
Every driver undergoes KYC (Know Your Customer) verification with ID upload and admin approval.

🗺️ Smart Proximity Matching
Our algorithm shows drivers deliveries within 1–5 km of their current location.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💰 Secure Payments
MOOVIZ does not process payments — payment is directly between sender and driver via:
• Bit
• PayBox
• Bank Transfer

Both parties confirm payment in-app to complete the transaction.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🇮🇱 Hebrew-First
Full Hebrew interface with RTL support. English also supported.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📞 Support
Having an issue? Contact us anytime through the app or via email.

Download now and join the MOOVIZ community!
```
