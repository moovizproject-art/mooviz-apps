1 Risk Assessment Methodology

This risk assessment follows an industry-standard qualitative approach. Each identified risk is evaluated on two dimensions: Likelihood (probability of occurrence) and Impact (severity of consequence). The product of these two values determines the overall Risk Score.

Likelihood Scale

2UnlikelyCould occur at some time but not expected

4LikelyWill probably occur in most circumstances

4 Risk Treatment Summary

Each identified risk is assigned a treatment strategy based on its residual risk rating and the cost-benefit of available controls.

Accept

R13 (App reverse engineering) — Server-side validation ensures no sensitive logic is exposed client-side. The residual risk is low and acceptable for an MVP.

R12 (Unauthorized file access) — Storage rules with participant checks provide adequate protection. Score of 3 is within tolerance.

Mitigate

All remaining risks (R1-R11, R14, R15) have controls implemented and additional mitigations planned. Key mitigations include:

  • Firebase App Check (R4, R6)
  • Biometric authentication (R1)
  • Automated npm audit in CI/CD (R10)
  • Privacy policy and DPA (R14)
  • Payment gateway integration (R11)

Transfer

No risks currently transferred. Recommendation: Consider cyber liability insurance to cover data breach costs (R2) and business interruption from service outages (R15).

Avoid

No risks avoided. All identified risks are inherent to the platform's core functionality and cannot be eliminated without removing essential features.

Key Observations

  • No critical or high residual risks remain after applying current controls.
  • Four medium risks require ongoing monitoring and planned mitigations.
  • Authentication (R1) is the highest-priority medium risk; biometric authentication is recommended for the next release.
  • Supply chain risk (R10) is common across all Node.js projects; automated auditing addresses this adequately.
  • Compliance risk (R14) requires legal counsel input for privacy policy and DPA finalization.
  • Payment fraud (R11) residual risk will decrease significantly with payment gateway integration in a future milestone.

Recommendation

The MOOVIZ platform demonstrates a mature security posture for an MVP-stage application. The overall residual risk is assessed as LOW. All critical security controls are in place, including server-side state machine validation, Firebase RBAC, encrypted storage, and comprehensive rate limiting. The four medium-residual risks have clear mitigation plans and do not represent blockers for production deployment. Regular reassessment is recommended on a quarterly basis.
